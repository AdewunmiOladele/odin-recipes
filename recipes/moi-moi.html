MOI MOI WITH FISH

Moi Moi with Fish is a delicious and nutritious Nigerian dish made with blended beans, fish, and aromatic spices. It's a steamed bean pudding that can be enjoyed as a main dish or side.

    INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups peeled and blended black-eyed peas
  • 1 cup flaked cooked fish (mackerel, tilapia, or any preferred fish)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 scotch bonnet peppers, blended (adjust to taste)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground crayfish
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 stock cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • Fish or vegetable broth (as needed)
  • Banana leaves or foil for wrapping

STEPS

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the blended black-eyed peas, chopped onions, minced garlic, blended scotch bonnet peppers, flaked cooked fish, ground crayfish, ground nutmeg, stock cubes, vegetable oil, and salt to taste.
  2. Gradually add fish or vegetable broth to achieve a smooth and thick batter consistency.
  3. Prepare banana leaves or foil for wrapping by cutting them into squares and briefly passing them over an open flame to make them pliable.
  4. Spoon the moi moi batter into the prepared leaves or foil, filling each about two-thirds full.
  5. Seal the leaves or foil packets, ensuring they are well-tied or folded to prevent water from entering during steaming.
  6. Place the wrapped moi moi in a steamer or a pot with a steamer rack. Steam for about 45-60 minutes or until firm and set.
  7. Allow the Moi Moi to cool a bit before unwrapping and serving.
  8. Serve Moi Moi with Fish as a main dish or as a side to rice, bread, or any accompaniment of your choice.
