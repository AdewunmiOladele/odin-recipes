CHICKEN PEANUT STEW

This hearty Chicken Peanut Stew is a delicious and comforting dish, combining tender chicken, vegetables, and a rich peanut sauce. Serve it over rice for a satisfying meal.

    INGREDIENTS

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup sweet potatoes, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

STEPS

  1. In a large pot, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until softened.
  2. Add minced garlic and chicken chunks. Brown the chicken on all sides.
  3. Stir in carrots, sweet potatoes, diced tomatoes, peanut butter, chicken broth, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring the stew to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer uncovered until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  5. Adjust seasoning if needed. Serve the stew over cooked rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.
